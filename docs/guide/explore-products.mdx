This guide explains how to browse Products on Prodfind.

## Using Home, Ranking, and New

1. Go to [Prodfind](https://prodfind.space).
2. Select **Home**, **Ranking**, or **New** from the sidebar to browse products.

## Using Explore

<Alert title="Caution" description="This method is available only when you are logged in." />

1. Go to the [Prodfind Dashboard](https://prodfind.space/dashboard).
2. Click the **Explore** tab to browse products.
